This is a name card and a set of stickers I designed to showcase my creativity and graphic design skills. The name card reflects a clean and elegant aesthetic with personalized elements, while the stickers convey a playful and expressive vibe. Each sticker design captures a unique character, making them versatile for various uses. This project allowed me to experiment with branding, visual consistency, and character illustration.

Tool(s) used: Illustrator and IbisPaint
